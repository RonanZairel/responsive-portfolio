Projects

Here are some of the projects I've worked on that reflect both functionality and user-centered design. Each project aims to solve real-world problems—whether it’s simplifying the borrowing of lab equipment or digitizing important health documents—while ensuring ease of use and accessibility.

Borrowlogy

Borrowlogy is a platform for university biology students to easily borrow lab materials. With Borrowlogy, students can quickly access the tools they need for their experiments and stay organized with a comprehensive history log to track all their borrowing activities.

E-VaxCa

E-VaxCa is an app mainly designed to combat wear and tear of paper-based vaccine cards, giving convenience to its users. Users with damaged or lost vaccine certificates may opt to use this app, and with its features, this will surely provide an easy process and access for its users' convenience.
